Custom Thief Skills

All skills start at 10% except climbing, which starts at 60%.

At character creation, the player distributes a pool of 60% to spend on thief skills. No more than 30% may be spent on any one skill.

At every subsequent level, the player gets a fresh pool of 30% to spend. No more than 15% may be spent on a single skill.

No skill may be increased beyond 95%.

Custom Magic User Spells

Magic users' spellbooks may contain practically unlimited spells. They start with a number of level one spells equal to three plus their intelligence modifier.

When magic users encounter new spells written on scrolls or spellbooks, they may attempt to transcribe them to their own spellbook. They must roll their intelligence score or less. A failure means that they cannot attempt to transcribe that spell until they encounter another unique instance on some other scroll or spellbook.
